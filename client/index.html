About Erica Wowo, at the age of 10 years, She had always dreamed of publishing a book, but every time she sat down to write, she found herself staring at a blank page, unable to put her thoughts into words. One day, while taking a walk in the park, Erica saw a butterfly struggling to break free from its cocoon. Moved by the tiny creature's determination, she decided to use its struggle as inspiration for her writing. She went home and wrote a story about a caterpillar who dreamed of flying but didn't know how to become a butterfly. Through a series of challenges and setbacks, the caterpillar discovered that the key to transformation was to embrace the struggle and keep pushing forward. To her surprise, Erica's story was picked up by a publisher and became a bestseller. From that day on, she never doubted her ability to write again.
